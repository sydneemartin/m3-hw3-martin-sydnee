Biography

Megan Thee Stallion, born Megan Pete, is an American rapper and singer who is from Houston, Texas. She was born on February 19, 1995 in San Antonio, Texas and began her career at the age of 20. She has won three Grammy Awards and many other accolades throughout her career.
